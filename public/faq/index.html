FAQ

Common questions.

What markets are covered?

The platform focuses on EV equities, technology ETFs, digital assets, and energy infrastructure themes.

Can I track account activity?

Yes. The dashboard presents deposits, active investments, realized profit, and available balance in one view.

Are withdrawals supported?

Yes. Account withdrawals can be requested from the investor dashboard.

Is identity review available?

Yes. Account review fields are available after registration.

Is email confirmation used?

Yes. Email confirmation helps secure account access and sensitive account actions.

Can I use crypto assets?

Digital-asset support is available alongside traditional account activity.

Does the dashboard work on mobile?

Yes. The layout is designed to stay clean across desktop and mobile screens.

How do I reach support?

Use the contact page or the floating support button when available.
